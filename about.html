Committed to Quality Real Estate Investments

Windy Point was formed in 2000 and is primarily focused on investments in commercial real estate. The company believes in long-term investing, holding a portfolio of multifamily, retail, and office properties in the Pacific Northwest. In 2008, Windy Point Consulting was created to provide management consulting and strategic advice with a focus on businesses in transition.

Investment Philosophy

Windy Point is a private investment concern. The company is managed by a small staff and relies significantly on outside asset and property managers. Over its twenty-year history, the Company has built a diversified portfolio of high-quality properties. The three fundamental pillars supporting Windy Point’s investment strategy can be summarized as:


Length – seeking positive long-term outcomes

Depth – strength and substance developed from effort, experience, and integrity

Diversity – to enhance results and mitigate risk

In all investment categories, Windy Point emphasizes these same fundamentals.


By its nature, investing involves risk. The company looks for investment opportunities where the balance between risk and reward is appropriate. Conversely, Windy Point avoids situations where risk cannot be accurately measured or priced. Risk measurement and management are pervasive and active. From an opportunistic perspective, the company will consider investments with higher risk profiles if the investment fits with the overall balance of the portfolio and can improve risk-adjusted returns.


The investment portfolio has been a meaningful beneficiary of “steady as she goes.” It is expected that the principles that have successfully guided Windy Point to date will remain in place for the foreseeable future.
